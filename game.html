Situation 0 (Question on Renting an Apartment):

The year is 2022.

You have 4,000 CAD$ in your bank account and you have successfully graduated from university. You are offered a sales internship at a logistics brokerage company named “Tremblay Fisher Hill Logistics”, one of Canada’s biggest logistics brokerage companies. You accept this offer because you think it will be a great learning opportunity.

You will be moving to Montreal, Quebec for this job and you are extremely excited about it. However, you need to rent a living space. Your monthly base salary is 2,500 CAD$, you visit several places and pick your top 3 apartments.

User must chose answer between the following three options:

A

Rent: 1050 CAD$, Distance to work: 7-minute walk, Size: 850 sq ft

B

Rent: 700 CAD$, Distance to work: 10-minute bus ride + 5-minute walk, Size: 725 sq ft

C

Rent: 500 CAD$, Distance to work: 35-minute bus ride +4-minute walk, Size: 600 sq ft
